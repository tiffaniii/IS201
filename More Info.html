    A little more about my role:
    My role in the HR Office at the MTC is HR Generalist 1. I work 3/4 time, which is the equivalent of about 30 hours per week. This is a balancing act for me as a full-time student.

  1. A few things that I focus on in my office include
    • Facilitating and coordinating the hiring efforts for the MTC.
      • Most specifically, I am responsible for the remote hires, including the coordination efforts, execution, onboarding and training, etc.
    • Assisting the other HR Generalist business partners
    • Merit increases for the entire part-time population of the MTC (give or take 1500 part time employees at any given time)
    • Reorganizing teams within the MTC
  2. Recently, the MTC had some huge changes, where we separated from BYU and transitioned every employee to be a church employee. Here are a few of my contributions to this transition:
    • An early transition to church employment to be able to learn the processes and train coworkers
    • The termination and rehire of every single employee. This includes creating an offer letter for each individual and filling out I-9 paperwork with them. All while maintaining daily processes in the office during a busy hiring season (typical for the end of summer)
    • Providing many trainings and acting as quality control to ensure accuracy
The transition is still continuing daily. It has been such an increadible growth opportunity. Every day brings new surprises and we are constantly adapting.
